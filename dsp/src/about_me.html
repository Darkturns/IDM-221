Jonathan Duong

Artist, Storyteller, Student

An incurable dreamer, hopeless romantic, unapologetic otaku, and budding storyteller. I am a new transfer student here at Drexel majoring in IDM and minoring in Video Production. My hope is for my images and stories to have, at the very least, a lingering impact on someone, somewhere.

I am trying to add as much value to this world as I can and in the past, I have taken time off school, using it to work through childhood traumas and finding what I am passionate about. I am a self-taught photographer and editor specializing in Lightroom and Photoshop. My photos have garnered over one million impressions on Instagram and I can be found @darkturns.
