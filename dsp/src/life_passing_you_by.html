Life Passing You By

Vatican City

When I found out tripods were prohibited inside the Vatican Museum, I thought my vision for this shot had to be scraped. After checking-in my tripod at the door, I set out to find another way. I ended up macgyvering a stable platform, through a combination of using the railing and using my camera strap as a brace. Standing still as stone, I managed to get my shutter speed down to a few seconds, all pretty much handheld. This allowed for the visitors to have the motion-blurred effect.
